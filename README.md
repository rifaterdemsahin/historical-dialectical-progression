# Historical Dialectical Progression

> https://rifaterdemsahin.github.io/historical-dialectical-progression/

## Overview

An interactive visualization of Marxist Historical Materialism, demonstrating the dialectical progression of human societies through five distinct historical stages. This project explores the evolution of economic systems, class conflicts, and the transformation of property relations throughout history.

## Educational Context

This visualization is based on Karl Marx's theory of historical materialism, which posits that the material conditions of a society's mode of production fundamentally determine its organization and development. Each stage represents a distinct mode of production characterized by:

- **Means of Production**: The tools and systems used to produce goods
- **Relations of Production**: The social relationships people enter into as they produce
- **Class Conflict**: The contradiction between classes that drives historical change
